<h1 class="text-3xl font-bold text-amber-600 mb-4">SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL) 2026 Syllabus</h1>

<p class="text-sm text-gray-500 mb-6">Syllabus • 08 June 2026</p>

<div class="bg-amber-50 dark:bg-gray-800 border-l-4 border-amber-500 p-4 rounded-lg shadow mb-6">

  <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold mb-2">Exam Pattern Overview</h2>

  <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 whitespace-pre-line">
    The SSC CGL 2026 selection process comprises two stages:
  </p>

  <h3 class="font-semibold">📘 Tier-I Examination</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li><strong>Mode:</strong> Online (Computer-Based Test)</li>
    <li><strong>Total Marks:</strong> 200</li>
    <li><strong>Total Questions:</strong> 100</li>
    <li><strong>Duration:</strong> 60 Minutes</li>
    <li><strong>Negative Marking:</strong> 0.50 Marks for each incorrect answer</li>
    <li><strong>Sections:</strong>
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
        <li>General Intelligence & Reasoning</li>
        <li>General Awareness</li>
        <li>Quantitative Aptitude</li>
        <li>English Comprehension</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">📗 Tier-II Examination</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li><strong>Mode:</strong> Online (Computer-Based Test)</li>
    <li><strong>Paper:</strong> Paper-I (Compulsory for all candidates)</li>
    <li><strong>Sections:</strong>
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
        <li>Mathematical Abilities</li>
        <li>Reasoning & General Intelligence</li>
        <li>English Language & Comprehension</li>
        <li>General Awareness</li>
        <li>Computer Knowledge Test</li>
        <li>Data Entry Speed Test (DEST)</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li><strong>Negative Marking:</strong> Applicable as per SSC rules</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 class="font-semibold mt-6">📚 Syllabus Highlights</h2>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">🎓 Quantitative Aptitude / Mathematical Abilities</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Number System</li>
    <li>Whole Numbers, Decimals & Fractions</li>
    <li>LCM & HCF</li>
    <li>Ratio & Proportion</li>
    <li>Percentage</li>
    <li>Average</li>
    <li>Profit & Loss</li>
    <li>Simple & Compound Interest</li>
    <li>Time & Work</li>
    <li>Time & Distance</li>
    <li>Partnership</li>
    <li>Algebra</li>
    <li>Geometry</li>
    <li>Mensuration</li>
    <li>Trigonometry</li>
    <li>Statistics</li>
    <li>Data Interpretation</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">🧠 General Intelligence & Reasoning</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Analogies</li>
    <li>Classification</li>
    <li>Coding-Decoding</li>
    <li>Number & Alphabet Series</li>
    <li>Blood Relations</li>
    <li>Direction Sense Test</li>
    <li>Venn Diagrams</li>
    <li>Syllogism</li>
    <li>Statement & Conclusion</li>
    <li>Decision Making</li>
    <li>Problem Solving</li>
    <li>Spatial Visualization</li>
    <li>Visual Memory</li>
    <li>Non-Verbal Reasoning</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">🌍 General Awareness</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Current Affairs</li>
    <li>Indian History</li>
    <li>Indian Geography</li>
    <li>Indian Polity & Constitution</li>
    <li>Indian Economy</li>
    <li>General Science</li>
    <li>Environment & Ecology</li>
    <li>Sports</li>
    <li>Awards & Honours</li>
    <li>Books & Authors</li>
    <li>Important Days & Events</li>
    <li>Government Schemes</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">📖 English Language & Comprehension</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Reading Comprehension</li>
    <li>Error Spotting</li>
    <li>Sentence Improvement</li>
    <li>Fill in the Blanks</li>
    <li>Cloze Test</li>
    <li>Para Jumbles</li>
    <li>Synonyms & Antonyms</li>
    <li>One Word Substitution</li>
    <li>Idioms & Phrases</li>
    <li>Active & Passive Voice</li>
    <li>Direct & Indirect Speech</li>
    <li>Vocabulary Usage</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">💻 Computer Knowledge Test</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Computer Fundamentals</li>
    <li>Hardware & Software</li>
    <li>Operating Systems</li>
    <li>MS Word, Excel & PowerPoint</li>
    <li>Internet & Networking</li>
    <li>Cyber Security</li>
    <li>Digital Literacy</li>
    <li>E-Governance</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">⌨️ Data Entry Speed Test (DEST)</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Qualifying in nature.</li>
    <li>Typing/Data Entry Test conducted for specified posts.</li>
    <li>Candidates must achieve the prescribed speed as notified by SSC.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">🧾 Document Verification</h3>
  <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Candidates qualifying Tier-II will be called for Document Verification.</li>
    <li>Original certificates and eligibility documents will be verified.</li>
    <li>Final selection will be based on merit and successful verification.</li>
  </ul>
