  <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold mb-2">Overview</h2>  
  <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">  
    The <strong>Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB)</strong> has released its official notification for the <strong>Group 2 Subgroup 3 Recruitment 2025</strong>. This recruitment drive is for multiple graduate-level government posts across 28 departments in Madhya Pradesh. According to different sources, the total number of vacancies ranges from 339 to 454
    The online application window is open — this is a key opportunity for candidates with a bachelor’s degree to secure a role in state administration.  
  </p>

        <li>Maximum Age: 40 years for General / EWS; in some sources, up to 45 years for OBC / SC / ST / PwD / Female. </li>  
        <li>Age relaxation is provided as per MP Government norms.</li>  
      </ul>  
      <h3 class="font-semibold mb-2">Residency / Nationality:</h3>  
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">

        <li>The test will be conducted in two shifts: Morning (09:00–12:00) and Afternoon (15:00–18:00) on the exam date.</li>  
        <li>Language of the paper: Likely Hindi & English (as is common in MPESB recruitments). Check the notification to confirm.</li>

    <div class="bg-amber-500 px-4">  
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white py-4">How to Apply</h2>  
    </div>  
    <div class="p-4">  
      <ol class="list-decimal pl-6 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">  
        <li>Visit the MPESB official portal: <a href="https://esb.mp.gov.in" class="text-blue-600">esb.mp.gov.in</a></li>  
        <li>Navigate to the “Recruitment / Group-2 Subgroup-3” section.</li>  
        <li>Read the full notification PDF carefully.</li>  
        <li>Register or log in with your MPOnline / MPESB credentials.</li>  
        <li>Fill in the online application form: personal details, educational qualifications, category, etc.</li>  
        <li>Upload required documents: passport-size photograph, signature, education certificates, category certificate (if applicable)./li>  
        <li>Pay the application fee using online payment methods or via MPOnline Kiosk.</li>  
        <li>Submit the form and take a printout of the confirmation page for records.</li>  
        <li>If needed, use the correction window (9 Sept – 28 Sept) to edit the application.</li>  
      </ol>  
    </div>  
  </div>

        Here are some useful tips for aspirants preparing for the MPESB Group 2 Subgroup 3 exam:  
      </p>  
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">  
        <li><strong>Understand the syllabus:</strong> Get clarity on topics for general knowledge, science, mathematics, and subject-specific (engineering, biology) content.</li>
