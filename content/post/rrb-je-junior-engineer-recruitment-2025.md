<div class="bg-amber-50 dark:bg-gray-800 border-l-4 border-amber-500 p-4 rounded-lg shadow mb-6">
  <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold mb-2">Overview</h2>
  <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
    The <strong>Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)</strong> has announced the much-awaited <strong>Junior Engineer (JE) Recruitment 2025</strong> under the Centralized Employment Notification (CEN) No. 05/2025. This recruitment aims to fill a total of <strong>2570 vacancies</strong> across various Railway Zones in India for the posts of <strong>Junior Engineer (JE)</strong>, <strong>Depot Material Superintendent (DMS)</strong>, and <strong>Chemical & Metallurgical Assistant (CMA)</strong>. Candidates with a diploma or degree in engineering disciplines are eligible to apply. Interested applicants must read the full notification available on <a href="https:// www.rrbcdg.gov.in" class="text-blue-600">www.rrbcdg.gov.in</a> before applying online through the respective RRB’s official website.
  </p>
</div>

      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white py-4">Eligibility Criteria</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="p-4">
      <h3 class="font-semibold mb-2">Educational Qualification:</h3>
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">
        Candidates must have a <strong>Diploma or Degree in Engineering</strong> from a recognized university or institution in the relevant trade. Detailed discipline-wise qualifications are listed in the official notification. Applicants must ensure that they meet the required academic standards before applying.
      </p>
      <h3 class="font-semibold mb-2">Age Limit (as on 01 January 2025):</h3>
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">
        <li>Minimum Age: 18 years</li>
        <li>Maximum Age: 36 years</li>
        <li>Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/OBC/PwBD and other categories as per government rules.</li>
      </ul>
      <h3 class="font-semibold mb-2">Nationality:</h3>
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
        Applicants must be citizens of India. Candidates from Nepal, Bhutan, or Tibetan refugees who came to India before 1st January 1962 may also apply as per eligibility rules.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="mb-8">
  <div class="bg-white dark:bg-gray-900 shadow rounded-lg overflow-hidden">
    <div class="bg-amber-500 px-4">
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white py-4">Selection Process & Exam Pattern</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="p-4">
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">
        The RRB JE 2025 selection process involves multiple stages of testing to ensure that only the most qualified candidates are selected. The process includes the following:
      </p>
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
        <li>Computer Based Test (CBT) – Stage I</li>
        <li>Computer Based Test (CBT) – Stage II</li>
        <li>Document Verification</li>
        <li>Medical Examination</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="mt-4 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
        The CBT will test candidates on subjects like General Awareness, General Science, Mathematics, Reasoning, and Technical Abilities. Each wrong answer will attract a penalty of <strong>1/3 mark</strong>. Candidates qualifying in CBT-1 will be shortlisted for CBT-2. Final selection will be based on merit and medical fitness.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="mb-8">
  <div class="bg-white dark:bg-gray-900 shadow rounded-lg overflow-hidden">
    <div class="bg-amber-500 px-4">
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white py-4">Salary & Benefits</h2>
    </div>
    <div class="p-4">
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
        The <strong>RRB Junior Engineer</strong> post falls under <strong>Level 6</strong> of the 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC). The basic pay is ₹35,400/- per month along with other allowances such as Dearness Allowance (DA), Transport Allowance, and House Rent Allowance (HRA). The in-hand salary generally ranges between ₹48,000 to ₹52,000 per month depending on posting location and allowances. This job provides strong career growth opportunities, job security, and pension benefits after retirement.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
