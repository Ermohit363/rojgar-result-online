Candidates can apply online through the UPSC portal before the last date.

      <p><strong>Educational Qualification:</strong></p>
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6">
        <li>Army Wing: Passed or appearing in Class 12 from a recognized board.</li>
        <li>Air Force, Navy & Naval Academy: Passed or appearing in Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM).</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>Age Limit:</strong> Candidates must be born between 02 January 2008 and 01 January 2011 (both dates inclusive).</p>
      <p><strong>Marital Status:</strong> Only unmarried male and female candidates are eligible.</p>
