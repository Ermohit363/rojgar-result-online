  <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">

    These vacancies are spread across diverse **streams** including <em>General, Legal, Information Technology, Research, Official Language, Electrical Engineering, and Civil Engineering</em>. 
    The detailed notification will be available on SEBI’s official website, and the online application window opens on **30 October 2025**.

      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white py-4">Eligibility Criteria</h2>  
    </div>  
    <div class="p-4">  
      <h3 class="font-semibold mb-2">Educational Qualification:</h3>  
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">  
        The required qualification depends on the stream:  
      </p>  
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">  
        <li><strong>General Stream:</strong> Master’s degree or a 2-year Postgraduate Diploma in any discipline, OR Bachelor’s degree in Law, Engineering, CA, CS, or Cost Accountant.</li>  
        <li><strong>Legal Stream:</strong> Bachelor of Laws (LLB).</li>  
        <li><strong>IT Stream:</strong> Bachelor’s in Engineering (Computer Science / IT) or a relevant degree. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}</li>  
        <li><strong>Research Stream:</strong> Master’s (or equivalent) in Economics, Statistics, Commerce, Business Administration, or related fields.</li>  
        <li><strong>Official Language:</strong> Postgraduate degree in Hindi, Sanskrit, English, or Economics / Commerce with Hindi, depending on the post.</li>  
        <li><strong>Engineering (Electrical / Civil):</strong> Bachelor’s degree in the corresponding engineering discipline.</li>  
      </ul>  
      <h3 class="font-semibold mb-2">Age Limit:</h3>  
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">  
        Maximum **30 years** as on 30 September 2025.
      </p>  
      <h3 class="font-semibold mb-2">Other Requirements:</h3>  
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">  
        Nationality, domicile, and other criteria will be as per SEBI’s detailed notification. Candidates should check the official PDF for stream-wise and category-wise conditions.  
      </p>

<section class="mb-8">  
  <div class="bg-white dark:bg-gray-900 shadow rounded-lg overflow-hidden">  
    <div class="bg-amber-500 px-4">  
      <h2 class="text-lg font-semibold text-white py-4">Selection Process</h2>  
    </div>  
    <div class="p-4">  
      <p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">  
        The selection for SEBI Assistant Manager (Grade A) posts will be conducted in **three phases**: 
      </p>  
      <ul class="list-disc pl-6 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">  
        <li>Phase I: Online Exam (2 papers)</li>  
        <li>Phase II: Online Exam (stream-specific)</li>  
        <li>Phase III: Interview for final selection</li>  
      </ul>  
      <p class="mt-3 text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">  
        In Phase I, Paper 1 will cover General Awareness (including financial sector), English, Quantitative Aptitude, and Reasoning. Phase II’s second paper will be related to the candidate’s chosen stream.
        Note: There is negative marking in Phase I (¼ mark for each wrong answer) in both papers.
      </p>  
    </div>  
  </div>  
</section>
