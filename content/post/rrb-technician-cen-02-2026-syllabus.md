<h2 class="text-lg font-semibold mb-2">Exam Pattern Overview</h2>

<p class="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 whitespace-pre-line">
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) conducts a Computer Based Test (CBT) for Technician Grade I Signal and Technician Grade III posts. Candidates should understand the latest exam pattern and syllabus before starting their preparation.
</p>

<h3 class="font-semibold">🚆 Technician Grade I Signal CBT</h3>

<ul class="list-disc pl-6">
<li><strong>Mode:</strong> Computer Based Test (CBT)</li>
<li><strong>Total Questions:</strong> 100</li>
<li><strong>Total Marks:</strong> 100</li>
<li><strong>Duration:</strong> 90 Minutes</li>
<li><strong>Negative Marking:</strong> 1/3 Mark Deducted for Every Wrong Answer</li>
<li><strong>Sections:</strong>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6">
<li>General Awareness – 10 Questions</li>
<li>General Intelligence & Reasoning – 15 Questions</li>
<li>Basics of Computers & Applications – 20 Questions</li>
<li>Mathematics – 20 Questions</li>
<li>Basic Science & Engineering – 35 Questions</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="font-semibold mt-4">🚆 Technician Grade III CBT</h3>

<ul class="list-disc pl-6">
<li><strong>Mode:</strong> Computer Based Test (CBT)</li>
<li><strong>Total Questions:</strong> 100</li>
<li><strong>Total Marks:</strong> 100</li>
<li><strong>Duration:</strong> 90 Minutes</li>
<li><strong>Negative Marking:</strong> 1/3 Mark Deducted for Every Wrong Answer</li>
<li><strong>Sections:</strong>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6">
<li>Mathematics – 25 Questions</li>
<li>General Intelligence & Reasoning – 25 Questions</li>
<li>General Science – 40 Questions</li>
<li>General Awareness – 10 Questions</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
